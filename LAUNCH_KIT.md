## 2. Stripe — sponsor invoicing (you already have Premium links live)

Your Premium checkout links are already wired into the app. To invoice **sponsors**:

1. Stripe Dashboard → **Products** → add product **"ParkEasy Sponsor Slot — Monthly"**, price **£30/month recurring** (or £25–40 per deal).
2. **Customers → add customer** (the business), then **Create invoice** → add the product → enable "first month free" with a 100%-off coupon for the first cycle.
3. Stripe emails them a hosted invoice + card link. Done — no code needed.

(When you sign a sponsor, tell me their name/area/offer and I'll wire their card into the live "Sponsored" slot.)
